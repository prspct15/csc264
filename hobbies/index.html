Sim Racing

My absolute favourite hobby, ever?

Twitch Channel

This is a weird one. It dated back when I started to like racing in general, like F1 and all other motorsports. When quarantine started, I took my controller and start playing racing games. To my surprise, I was pretty fast, like pretty damn fast. So I started to race semi-competitively and eventually got the eyes of some people in the community.

And until earlier this year they decided, "Lets just get him a wheel, he has potential", and here I am today with a shiny new wheel, which I absolutely love it. And with that too I am more dedicated and motivated in this field.

Only recently I have dipped my toes into actual racing, well karting. It definitely is a different feel compared to the stationary seated sim environment. The G-forces pushing and pulling me all over the track is a unique experience that I am looking forward more of. Simultaneously too my respect for drivers have grown significantly. They are mad. Like mad mad.
